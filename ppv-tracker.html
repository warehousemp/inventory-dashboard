Extra Completed Visits

Use this only when a route tech completed actual cleanings that were not included in the Skimmer Tech Summary import. This saves an approved CORRECTION with a visit adjustment count.

Manual Dollar Adjustment

Use this only for approved payroll dollar corrections that are not visit credits, PTO/holiday/other time, quarterly commissions, or safeguard events. Positive and negative amounts are allowed.
If there are no extra cleanings or manual dollar adjustments for this pay period, use the review-complete button to move forward.

Extra RT Meeting / Maintenance Credits

Enter approved meeting or maintenance credit units. Decimals are allowed and are paid at the pickup visit rate.
Select a route tech and enter approved credits to preview pay impact.

Temporary Safeguard Entry

This is a temporary admin entry workflow until the Route Tech Leader safeguard workflow is built. Failed QC and causation callback penalties should be capped according to company policy before saving.
Select a route tech and safeguard type to preview the calculated penalty.

Quarterly Commission Earned

Enter approved quarterly commission earned for a route tech. This is separate from visit corrections, paid-time credits, and safeguards.

HR Hours / PTO / Holiday Pay Review

Internal payroll review only. Regular/overtime hours are used for minimum-wage/effective-hourly review. PTO, holiday, and other time convert to visit-equivalent credits at 1.5 visits per hour. PTO/holiday credits pay at the final PPV rate. Other time credits pay at the pickup visit rate.
Select a route tech to preview HR hours, visit credits, and effective hourly review.
