Pool / Spa Equipment Inspection Request

Use this form to request a Pool / Spa Equipment Inspection from Molinari Pools. The office will review your request and contact you to confirm scheduling.

Important Inspection Notice This request form is for pool, spa, and accessible equipment inspection scheduling. The inspection report is separate from any repair or replacement quote. Final findings, recommendations, photos, and pricing will only be provided to the authorized client or authorized client representative after office review.

1. Requester Information

Tell us who is submitting this inspection request.

2. Who Is the Inspection For?

This controls who is authorized to receive findings, photos, recommendations, and pricing.

3. Property Information

Provide the address where the pool/spa equipment inspection will be completed.

4. Real Estate Transaction Information

Complete this section if the inspection is related to a purchase, sale, or closing deadline.

5. Access Information

Provide the best access contact and any gate, lockbox, or scheduling instructions.

6. Pool / Spa Information

Share what you know about the pool, spa, equipment, and any current concerns.

7. Authorized Report Recipient

Molinari Pools will only provide inspection findings, photos, recommendations, and pricing to the authorized client or authorized client representative.

8. Quote / Follow-Up Preference

The inspection report and repair quote are separate. The office will review inspection findings before sending any quote.

9. Required Acknowledgements

Please review and confirm before submitting your request.
